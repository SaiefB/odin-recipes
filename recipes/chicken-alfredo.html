Description

Delight in the creamy decadence of our Chicken Alfredo. This classic dish features tender, juicy slices of grilled chicken breast nestled atop a bed of perfectly cooked fettuccine pasta. Each strand of pasta is generously coated in a rich and velvety Alfredo sauce, made from a luxurious blend of butter, heavy cream, and freshly grated Parmesan cheese. Infused with a hint of garlic and a sprinkle of black pepper, this sauce offers a luscious, comforting flavor in every bite. Garnished with fresh parsley and served hot, our Chicken Alfredo is the perfect indulgence for family dinners, romantic evenings, or any occasion that calls for a touch of elegance and comfort.

  • 1 pound uncooked fettuccine or linguine
  • 2 boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cubed
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar Alfredo-style pasta sauce/li>
  • 1 (10 ounce) package frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 (4.5 ounce) can sliced mushrooms
  • â…“ cup milk

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Cook fettuccine at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain well.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, place cubed cooked chicken, Alfredo sauce, frozen vegetables, mushrooms, and milk in a large saucepan over medium-low heat. Cook and stir until chicken is heated through and vegetables are tender.
  3. Serve warm Alfredo and chicken sauce over cooked noodles.
